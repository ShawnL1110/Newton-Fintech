About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2017, Newton Fintech has grown into a leading fintech company operating across Southeast Asia and Latin America, with headquarters in Barcelona, Spain.

Our core offerings include neobanking and consumer credit solutions, enhanced by the power of artificial intelligence. As one of the fastest-growing fintech companies in Asia, we are also one of the few to provide multinational mobile personal finance services.

Today, we operate offices in 7 countries, serving millions of users with innovative financial products that combine local market expertise with world-class AI technology.
